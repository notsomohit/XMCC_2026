About XIM University

XIM University, Bhubaneswar, embodies the philosophy of “Inspiring Futures” ; nurturing young minds to innovate, lead, and pursue new paths. Committed to societal progress and sustainable development, the university serves as a catalyst for change across Odisha and India. It offers diverse programs in Management, Law, Rural and Sustainability Studies, Communications, Engineering, Economics, Humanities, Compassion Studies, and Governance, preparing visionary and values-driven leaders. The experiential “learning by doing” model, supported by active student forums, fosters innovation, leadership, and personal growth.

About Xavier Law School

Xavier Law School, XIM University, is a leading new-age law institution recognized by the Bar Council of India. It is equipped with advanced physical, digital, and academic infrastructure, and is set amidst the greenery of suburban Bhubaneswar. The school is dedicated to shaping socially responsible and ethically grounded legal professionals. Its academic strength is founded on rigorous teaching, research, and practice-oriented learning. Students receive comprehensive professional training through a digitally enabled Moot Court Hall, an active Legal Aid Clinic, and access to premier databases like SCC Online, Manupatra, and Lexis Advance.
